History

Techcon began in 2015 as a local gathering of Kenyan tech enthusiasts and has since grown into a regional conference across Africa bringing together thousands of attendees annually.

Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to inspire and empower the next generation of technology leaders through insightful discussions, workshops, and networking opportunities.

Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has hosted some of the most influential figures in the tech industry. Here are a few notable speakers:

  • Dr. Zuri Iruwa:A pioneer in AI research and robotics, she shared insights on the future of artificial intelligence & robotics and their potential impact on the growing African population

  • Mr. Hussein Ali: A Business Intelligence expert helping startups navigate the business aspect

  • Ms. Lulu Chang: An advocate for diversity in tech, Ms. Chang delivered a powerful keynote at TechCon 2020.
